About Us

Gilly Sibambo Holdings was established in 2021 and is owned and run by black youth. The company focuses on empowering the youth and women while offering 360-degree sourcing, supply, and delivery solutions with a core focus on branding and signage.

Our Mission

To facilitate greater black economic participation in South Africa’s economic evolution through excellent service in our professional disciplines. We are committed to visionary leadership, skilled management, and project completion within specified time frames.

Our Vision

To provide exceedingly superior services that satisfy our customer needs and contribute to the growth of South Africa’s GDP.
